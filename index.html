Who We Are

About Insika

Insika Foundation NPC was founded in 2012 and is a 100% Black and majority female owned entity. Currently Insika Foundation has a level 1 BBBEE score and has a footprint in Gauteng, Kwa Zulu Natal and the Western Cape. As a Not for Profit Organisation (NPO – /08-NPO Number 183-347) Insika connects business, government, and the community through social projects in the following key programmes:

- Community Work Programme (CWP)
- Internships
- Training
- Corporate Social Investment (CSI)

The Insika Foundation Promise

Insika Foundation empowers beneficiaries with tangible life skills. We appreciate that not all beneficiaries necessarily require the same thing – some people require a computer course, some a driver’s license and others may require some workplace experience. Our objective is to connect our beneficiaries based on their individual needs moving them from point A to B.

The Insika Foundation Achievements

City of Johannesburg, certificate of excellence, 2016.
Gauteng Premier awards, 2019.

The Insika Foundation Journey

Insika Foundation started in Gauteng and then grew to Kwazulu-Natal and the Western Cape. Initially we focused on community work but we have moved into training, internships, Learnerships, community work and motivational talks.
