Internships

About Our Internships

An internship is structured and monitored work assignments so that a learner/intern can gain real workplace experience through exposure and interactions within a real work environment.

Available Fields

  • Human Resource Management
  • Marketing Management
  • Business Management
  • Financial Management

  • Tourism Management
  • Hospitality
  • Travel Management

Administration & Public Service

  • Office Administration
  • Public Relations
  • Public Management
  • Management Assistance

How to Apply

To apply for an internship, please send the following documents to recruitment@insikafoundation.co.za:

  • Curriculum Vitae (CV)
  • Certified copies of your qualifications
  • Certified copy of your ID
  • Proof of Address
