About Our KZN Office

Insika Foundation has a provincial office set up in Umhlanga, Kwa-Zulu Natal in order to serve the province at a local level. The operational model enables our staff, Provincial Managers, Site Managers and Site Administrators to reach local communities more efficiently, especially in remote, underserved areas of the province.


The Kwa-Zulu Natal office is managed by Nompumelelo Ngubane (Provincial Manager) and she is supported by ten staff members.

Flagship Projects

Makhosaneni Piggery

Started in April 2019 at Okhahlamba to connect local farmers to mainstream farming operations. The project assists participants with processing of agricultural products, marketing, and insurance for farming businesses. Thirty-three (33) CWP beneficiaries are currently part of this programme.


Zizamele Bakery

Located at Mhlabuyalingana, this project was initiated in April 2019 and currently has 18 CWP participants as beneficiaries. The objective is to uplift small businesses in rural and underdeveloped areas by creating sustainable employment opportunities.

Bringing sponsors and beneficiaries together to ensure sustainable results in all Insika programmes.
