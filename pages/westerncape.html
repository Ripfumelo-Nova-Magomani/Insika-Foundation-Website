About Our Western Cape Office

Insika Foundation has a provincial office set up in Somerset West, Western Cape in order to serve the province at a local level.
The operational model enables our staff, Provincial Managers, Site Managers and Site Administrators to reach local communities more efficiently, especially in remote, underserved areas of the province.


In the implementation of Insika Foundation projects and programmes, the objective is to use regional knowledge and expertise to roll-out initiatives in the most effective way working in collaboration with the community, municipality and other local structures.

Flagship Projects

Hair, Nail and Beauty Salon Programme

Started in March 2019, this programme aims to create SMME opportunities for CWP participants. The participants receive technical training in hair, nail technology, and beauty technology, along with business skills for successful program exit. Currently, 25 beneficiaries are participating in this transformative initiative.

Support to the Education Sector

Initiated in March 2012, this programme supports under-resourced schools and ECD centres in delivering quality education to disadvantaged communities. Participants serve as classroom assistants, tutors, sports coaches, library assistants, and administrative clerks. The programme has benefited over 8,000 learners across the province, significantly improving academic performance and promoting early reading culture.

Bringing sponsors and beneficiaries together to ensure sustainable results in all Insika programmes.
