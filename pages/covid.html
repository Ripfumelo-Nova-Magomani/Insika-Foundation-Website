Our Commitment to Safety

Insika Foundation is dedicated to maintaining a safe and healthy work environment for all employees. We have implemented comprehensive measures to prevent the spread of COVID-19 while ensuring continuous operations.

Social Distancing

Maintaining appropriate physical distance between individuals to minimize the risk of viral transmission.

Symptom Screening

Regular health checks and temperature monitoring for all employees and visitors.

Sanitizer Stations

Mandatory use of hand sanitizers with strategically placed stations throughout the facility.

Workspace Disinfection

Regular and thorough disinfection of all work areas to maintain a clean environment.

Mandatory Masks

Required use of face masks by all employees and visitors while on premises.

PPE Provision

Complete Personal Protective Equipment provided to all Insika Foundation staff.

For the latest COVID-19 information and updates, please visit www.sacoronavirus.co.za
