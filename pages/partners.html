Working Together for Change

Insika Foundation has various partners both locally and internationally across a range of different projects and project objectives.

ZTE Corporation

Insika Foundation in partnership with ZTE Corporation championed a project supplying telecommunication equipment such as computers, tablets, projectors and routers for the training and development of rural communities. ZTE Corporation is based in China and is a global leader in telecommunications and information.

Teach in China

Insika Foundation has worked in partnership with the Chinese Culture and International Exchange Centre on the "Teach in China" initiative. The initiative was launched as a programme between South Africa and China recruiting English teachers for universities, high schools, primary schools and pre-schools in China. The programme also focuses on the cultural exchange between South Africa and China.

Third World Large Foundation

The Third World Large Foundation focuses on promoting social development and human rights progress through international cooperation. Key themes include:

  • Responsibility in promoting social development
  • Innovation in foundation practices
  • International cooperation among foundations
