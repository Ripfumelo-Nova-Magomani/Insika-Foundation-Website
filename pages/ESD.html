Cell C- Enterprise Supplier Development (ESD) Initiative

Insika Foundation in partnership with Cell C conducted nationwide workshops in all nine provinces in South Africa in support of Enterprise Supplier Development (ESD).

Main objectives of the workshops:

  • Improving BBBEE ratings
  • Improving SMME performance overall
  • Reducing costs
  • Resolving quality issues
  • Identifying and developing new business streams
  • Improving business alignment between the beneficiary and the sponsoring organisation
  • Developing a product or service that is not easily accessible in the market
  • Generating competition for a high price

Beneficiary selection included:

  • Small, Medium and Micro enterprises
  • Informal sectors
  • Rural entrepreneurs
  • Townships
  • Women
  • Historically Disadvantaged Individuals (HDIs)
  • People Living with Disabilities
