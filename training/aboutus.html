About Insika Training Academy

Insika FoundationTraining Academy remains committed to its discourse to develop, uplift, and inspire high quality skills much needed within our economic workforce. We equip participants within the sectors of Agriculture, Banking Transport, Fibre Processing and Manufacturing, Information Technology Systems, Education and Training, Cooking & Hospitality and Local Government.

Our training is recognized by Sector Education & Skills Training Authorities (SETAs) and industry regulators to ensure consistent quality output.
