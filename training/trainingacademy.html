Insika Foundation Training Academy

Insika Foundation Training Academy is a branch of the parent company known as Insika Foundation NPC. The Training Academy focuses specifically on skills development to improve one's employability. With the ever evolving skill advancement and aggressive economic exclusion, the Training Academy will ensure that you are always on top of your professional development. We pride ourselves on the life-changing skills we have transferred to all who have attended our training.
